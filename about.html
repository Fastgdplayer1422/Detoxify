Our Mission

At Detect & Detox, our mission is rooted in the belief that clean, safe food should be a right not a luxury. We are committed to providing a simple yet powerful way for people to protect their health and reduce their exposure to harmful substances through the foods they eat every day. Our approach is grounded in nature, using only plant-based and eco-friendly ingredients to ensure that you and your family can enjoy produce the way nature intended—fresh, clean, and free from invisible contaminants.

Why We Started

Modern fruits and vegetables may look fresh and vibrant, but they’re often coated in synthetic waxes, chemical preservatives, and traces of pesticides that aren’t visible to the naked eye. When we discovered just how persistent and overlooked these residues are, we realized that rinsing with water simply wasn’t enough. That’s when the idea for Detect & Detox was born—a safe, effective, and natural alternative that empowers people to take control of what they consume. We believe in transparency, and that starts by helping you see what’s really on your food.

What We Offer

Detect & Detox is a unique two-step cleaning system that helps you take the guesswork out of food safety. First, the DETECT spray uses plant-based ingredients to react with hidden layers of wax, pesticides, and chemicals—revealing them so you can actually see what needs to be cleaned. Then, our DETOX spray steps in, formulated with gentle yet effective natural cleansers to break down and wash away those harmful residues without affecting the taste, texture, or nutrients of your food. The result is produce that’s not only visibly cleaner, but truly safer to eat.

Our Values

We are driven by a deep respect for health, the environment, and ethical consumer choices. All of our ingredients are 100% biodegradable and non-toxic, ensuring they are safe not only for humans but also for waterways and wildlife. We believe in full transparency, which is why we show you what’s on your food before removing it. Our solutions are cruelty-free, chemical-free, and designed with minimal environmental impact in mind. With Detect & Detox, clean living becomes effortless and sustainable.

Designed for Everyday Life

Whether you’re a busy parent trying to give your kids healthier meals, a mindful eater focused on nutrition, or someone who simply wants peace of mind, Detect & Detox fits seamlessly into your routine. It’s easy to use, affordable, and built for daily life. We’re here to help you reclaim control over your food in the most natural way possible—because what you eat matters, and so does how it’s cleaned.
