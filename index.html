A Two-Step, All-Natural Cleaning System

Detect & Detox is a powerful, eco-conscious solution designed to make fruits and vegetables safer to eat. Using only plant-based ingredients, this two-step spray system helps protect your health by exposing and removing harmful substances all without harming your food or the planet.

Reveal What You Can’t See

Pesticides, synthetic waxes, and chemical residues are often invisible to the naked eye but that doesn’t mean they’re harmless. The DETECT spray uses natural ingredients that react with these hidden layers, causing them to become visible. With just a few sprays, you’ll see what’s really on your produce empowering you to take action before it reaches your plate.

Rinse Away the Harm

Once the residues are revealed, the DETOX spray gently but effectively breaks them down. Made from plant derived cleansers, this formula safely removes the unwanted layers without stripping nutrients or flavor. Your produce is left clean, fresh, and ready to enjoy.

Sustainable by Design

At Detect & Detox, we believe that caring for your health shouldn't come at the cost of the environment. That’s why every ingredient is:

  • Biodegradable
  • Non-toxic
  • Safe for waterways
